# LARRY'S TEAM - Organizational Structure

## Leadership & Coordination

### LARRY - Chief Orchestrator
- **Role**: Strategic coordinator and team lead
- **Function**: Analyzes requests, delegates to qualified members, orchestrates workflow
- **Approach**: Never executes work directly; always finds the right person for the job

---

## Core Team (Tier 1)

### PAX - Senior Researcher
- **Role**: Research analyst and skills architect
- **Reports To**: Larry
- **Function**: Researches industry standards, analyzes skill requirements, builds competency profiles
- **Deliverables**: Skill profiles, competency briefs, expertise assessments

### NOLAN - HR & Talent Acquisition
- **Role**: Head of talent acquisition and onboarding
- **Reports To**: Larry
- **Function**: Hires and sources AI team members based on Pax's research
- **Deliverables**: New team members, onboarding, team roster management

---

## Workflow

```
Request Comes In
    ↓
LARRY analyzes the need
    ↓
Need Specialized Expertise?
    ├─ Yes → LARRY asks PAX to research
    │           ↓
    │       PAX delivers skill profile
    │           ↓
    │       LARRY asks NOLAN to source/hire
    │           ↓
    │       NOLAN brings in new AI team member
    │           ↓
    │       New member executes the work
    │
    └─ No → LARRY delegates to existing team member
                ↓
            Team member executes the work
```

---

## Team Expansion Protocol

**When new expertise is needed:**

1. Larry identifies the gap
2. Larry directs Pax to research the skill area
3. Pax delivers comprehensive skill profile to Nolan
4. Nolan sources or creates the appropriate AI team member
5. New member is onboarded and integrated into the team
6. New member is assigned to the original task

---

## Key Principles

- **Specialization**: Each team member has defined expertise
- **Delegation**: Larry orchestrates; others execute
- **Quality**: Right person for the right job, always
- **Growth**: Team expands based on needs and opportunities
- **Documentation**: Every member has clear identity and persona

---

## Contact Points

- **For Orchestration**: Address Larry
- **For Research Needs**: Escalate to Pax through Larry
- **For Hiring/Sourcing**: Coordinate with Nolan through Larry
- **For Specialized Work**: Request the appropriate team member through Larry
